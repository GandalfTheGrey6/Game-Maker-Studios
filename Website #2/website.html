Tic-Tac-Toe

This brand new inovation from Game Makers Studio is going to revolutionize the field of gaming. Tic-Tac-Toe is a game comprising of control and invasion of squares in a 3 by 3 grid. Players take turns 'capturing' unoccupied squares until one play has managaed to win by connecting two corners or opposite sides with their domain. This is a modern day, minimalistic version of chess and makes an excellent jumping off point for tactical real-time and turn based strategy games. This engaging gameplay is augmented by excellent graphics and noise direction. Advanced shaders are used to give the lines and boxes a distinct flatness and simplicity that looks next-gen compared to games like Big Button and Rock, Paper, Scissors.
